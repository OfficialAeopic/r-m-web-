If you live in Cinco Ranch, Cross Creek Ranch, or anywhere around Katy, a little preparation makes tax season painless. Here is what to gather before your appointment.

## Income documents

Bring every income form you received: W-2s from employers, 1099-NEC for contract work, 1099-INT and 1099-DIV from banks and brokerages, and any 1099-K from payment apps. If you sold a home near Firethorne or Seven Meadows, bring the closing statement.

## Deduction and credit records

Mortgage interest (Form 1098), property taxes — which run high across Fort Bend and Harris County — charitable donations, medical expenses, and childcare costs. If you have dependents, have their Social Security numbers and dates of birth ready.

## Prior-year return

A copy of last year's return helps us catch carryovers and missed credits. New to R & M? We will review it as part of our [tax preparation](/services/tax-preparation) service.

## Bring questions

Buying a business, starting a side hustle, or unsure about an IRS notice? Ask. We are open year-round, not just January through April.
