If you run a business around Cypress — Bridgeland, Towne Lake, Fairfield — clean books are the difference between a calm tax season and a scramble. The question we hear most: how often do I really need bookkeeping done?

## The case for monthly

Monthly bookkeeping keeps your financials decision-ready. You catch problems early, your reports mean something, and lenders take you seriously. For businesses with payroll, inventory, or more than a handful of transactions a week, monthly is usually the right call.

## When quarterly is enough

A very small operation with few transactions can sometimes run on quarterly bookkeeping — provided nothing falls through the cracks. The risk is that small errors compound for three months before anyone notices.
