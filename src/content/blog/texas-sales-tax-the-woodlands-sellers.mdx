If you sell products — online or from a storefront near Market Street or Creekside Park — Texas sales and use tax is part of life. Here is the plain version.

## Do you owe it?

If you have nexus in Texas (a physical presence, or enough sales) and sell taxable goods, you generally must collect and remit sales tax. Services and some goods are exempt, so taxability is worth confirming rather than guessing.

## How often you file

The Comptroller assigns a filing frequency — monthly, quarterly, or annually — based on your volume. Miss a deadline and penalties and interest add up fast.
